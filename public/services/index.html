Racing Rules…

General Rules for Racing

NO SWEARING. This is a Family environment, Children are present.    
NO Food or Drinks in track area. They must stay in the pit/store area.
Cars will be replaced on track were they left the track.
If you are not racing you are expected to marshal.
NO non racers (excluding Marshal) are allowed on the track.
NO Substitute Drivers allowed.
All Drivers must check in at the main Register before racing.
Races need to start on time, There will be no waiting for drivers 
to “finish repairs.”
ALL Transmitters MUST be turned off if they are not being used in a race.
All cars must use a AMBrc Transponder.
Loaner Transponder are available on a limited basis.  It is  highly recommended  that you use a AMBrc Personal Transponder
All cars must pass a tech inspection when asked by the race director.   
All non-Spektrum Drivers must use a radio clip and be willing to use a different radio channel if asked by the race director.  Channel preference is decided by time of entry for that nights race.
Traction Compound can be used, Cans must be kept closed when not in use.  
NO OPEN Traction Compound is allow in the store area.
NO Pitting of cars in the store area.


1/18th Scale Brushed Class Rules

Any 2-wd or 4-wd 1/18 Scale Truck/Buggy chassis.
Any Open or Closed Wheel Body is allowed.
Any Commercially Available ESC is allowed.
Any Commercially Available Brushed Motor is allowed.
Maximum Battery size is 6-Cell 7.2V  1500mah max or 7.4 /2 cell 2100mah Lipo. 
Reverse is not Allowed.
Only smooth surface type tires are allowed.
Qualifier Rounds are 5 minutes in length.  
Mains are 5 minutes in length.

If enough racers are entered into this class that could form a seperate juniors/novice class on race night, they will be split off.


1/18th Scale Open Class Rules

Any 2-wd or 4-wd 1/18 Scale Truck/Buggy chassis.
Any Closed Wheel Body is allowed.
Any Commercially Available ESC is allowed.
Any Commercially Available Brushed or Brushless Motor is allowed.
There is no Maximum Battery size. 
Only smooth surface type tires are allowed.
Reverse is not Allowed.


1/10th Bolink/RC Spec Legend Class Rules
1/10th Bolink/RC Spec Legend Class Rules
Only Unmodified Bolink/RC Spec Legend chassis will be allowed.
Wheel Bearings may be used.
ROAR Legal 27t OR NOVAK 13.5 Brushless with 4 cell Nimh
21.5 Bl with 2s 7.4v ROAR approved Lipo
Gearing: Stock 81 tooth and choice of 21 or 22t pinion.
Only GREEN, SILVER, or BLUE compound pan car style wheels and tires can be used.
All cars must have a Minimum Ride Height of 5mm.
There is no minimum car weight.
All cars must use a front bumper.
All Bodies/Cars must be painted.
Each Body/Car must have a number(s) on each side and on the roof typical of a normal race car No car
number more than two digits in length.
Qualifiers are 5 minutes & Mains are 8 minutes in length.


1/10th Scale Touring Car Sportsman Class Rules
This Class is for drivers that are new to R/C Racing or need better car control.
Any 4-wd Touring Car Chassis.
Any 2 or 4 door Sedan body.
Wing must not be no higher than roof line.
7.2V 6 cell nimh/nicd battery 5300maH maximum or 2s (7.4v) ROAR approved Li-PO.
ROAR Spec 27Turn Stock Motor and 17.5 Brushless System.
The RTR Brushed Motor may be used on a limited bases but will need Race Director approval.
Any premounted rubber tire, Jaco Blues are highly recommended
The RTR Tires may be used but will need Race Director approval.
5mm Ride Height Minimum.
All Cars must meet a Minimum Weight of 1425g when using a 6 cell pack or Lipo Battery.
Qualifiers & Mains are 5 minutes in length.
If a Driver is dominating this class, They will be asked to move up to another class.
This is subject to the Race Director discretion.
Reverse is not Allowed.


1/10th Scale Touring Car Stock Rubber Class Rules
This Class is for drivers that are experience to R/C Racing but are not let to the expert level.
Any 4-wd Tour Car Chassis.
Any 2 or 4 door Sedan body.
Wing must not be no higher than roof line.
7.2V 6 cell nimh/nicad battery 4600maH maximum or 2 cell (7.4v) Li-PO battery pack ALL Lipo Packs must
be enclosed in a case.
Only ROAR approved 27 turn Stock motors and any ROAR approved 17.5 Brushless System
Only premount rubber tires, Jaco Blues are highly recommended
5mm Ride Height Minimum
All Cars must meet a Minimum Weight of 1425g.
If a Driver is dominating or hindering this class, They will be asked to move to another class.
This is subject to the Race Director discretion.
Qualifiers & Mains are 6 minutes in length.

Carpet Offroad Rules… 

SCT/Slash Rules

Stock Slash
Traxxas Slash, no after market chassis’s or chassis modifications.
Must use stock shocks or Traxxas shocks specifically intended for the Slash.
Radio gear may be changed, fluids may be changed,any SCT type tire/rim from any manufacturer.
Motors will be the stock Titan 12T, any ROAR 27T, or ROAR 17.5 Brushless motor. Variable timing
advance ESC’s are not allowed, ie, SPX, SXX, and Tekin RS/Pro.
Batteries will be limited to any 7.2v-8.4v, 6-7-cell, nimh, or 2s 7.4v ROAR approved lipo
Bodies must be SCT specific, and should be painted to replicate current or past vehicles.
Option parts will be limited to any part specifically designed for the Traxxas Slash produced by MIP, Traxxas, Integy or RPM.
Race Director has final say on legality.


SCT
Any SCT chassis
Batteries, will be same as Stock Slash.
Motors, will be 10.5 BL or Brushed equivalent.
Any SCT tire/ rim combo.
Open Offroad
This class is intended for those who are just starting to participate in a racing format and is open to all
offroad type vehicles. Ie, Stampede’s, Rustler’s, Evader’s, T4’s etc.


Race Format
Each race is comprised of 2 qualifying races of 20 laps, and mains are 25 laps of “heads up racing.”
Lower mains may be shortened.
Open offroad is also 2 qualifiers, but of 15 laps, and mains are 20 laps.
